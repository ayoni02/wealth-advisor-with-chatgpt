Here are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Make a budget and stick to it.

2. Invest in yourself by taking courses and learning about financial planning and investing.

3. Start saving as early as possible. Invest in a 529 plan for your education costs.

4. Make sure you have an emergency fund to cover unexpected costs.

5. Invest money wisely to reach your goals. Consider a mix of different investment options to get the best return on your investment.
