Assuming this person wants comprehensive financial advice, here are a few steps he could take to improve his financial situation:

1. Save: One of the most important aspects of good financial planning is saving your money. Try to put away at least 10% of your monthly income into savings. This will help you in the long run, especially if you ever encounter unexpected expenses or financial setbacks.

2. Invest: Another key element of financial planning is investing your money. This can be done in a variety of ways, such as buying stocks, mutual funds, or bonds. investing your money can help you grow your wealth over time.

3. Create a budget: One way to help keep your finances in order is to create a budget. This will allow you to track your spending and make sure you are not spending more than you are earning. There are many budgeting tools available online or through financial planning software.

4. Reduce your expenses: If you find that you are spending more than you can afford, try to reduce your expenses. One way to do this is to cut back on non-essential items, such as eating out, entertainment, and shopping.

5. Make a plan: Having a financial plan can help you reach your financial goals. A financial planner can help you create a customized plan based on your specific circumstances and goals.
