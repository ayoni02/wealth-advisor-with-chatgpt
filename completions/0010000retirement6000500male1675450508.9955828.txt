It is important to save for retirement as early as possible.

There are a few things this person can do to start saving for retirement:

1. Invest in a 401k or other retirement account offered through their employer.

2. Begin contributing to an IRA.

3. Invest in a 529 plan for their children's education.

4. Make regular contributions to a savings account.

5. Invest in stocks, bonds, or other investments.

This person should also make sure to keep their monthly expenses low in order to have more money available to save for retirement. They may need to make some lifestyle changes such as downsizing their home or getting rid of unnecessary expenses.

It is also important to have a plan for what to do with the money once it is saved. This person should speak with a financial advisor to find the best way to invest their money for retirement.
