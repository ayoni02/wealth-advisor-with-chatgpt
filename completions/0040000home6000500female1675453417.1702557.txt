Assuming you would like tips for long term financial planning: 

1. Invest in yourself: One of the best things you can do for your future is to invest in yourself. Consider taking courses, saving for retirement, and increasing your earnings potential. 

2. Live below your means: One of the best ways to save money is to live below your means. Try to find ways to cut expenses and live within your means. 

3. Make a budget: One of the best ways to stay on track financially is to make a budget. Determine your income and expenses and track your spending. This will help you stay on track and make better financial decisions. 

4. Invest in assets: Another good way to prepare for your future is to invest in assets. Consider investing in stocks, bonds, and other assets that will increase in value over time. 

5. Save for your goals: One of the most important things you can do is to save for your goals. Whether you want to buy a house, retire early, or send your children to college, you need to save for it. Try to find ways to save money each month so you can reach your financial goals.
