Assuming you would like advice on how to improve the person's financial situation: 

1. Invest in yourself: Education and job training can lead to higher earnings and more job security. Consider taking courses or becoming certified in your field to make yourself more marketable. 

2. Make a budget and stick to it: Track your income and expenses so you know where your money is going. Make sure your monthly expenses do not exceed your income. 

3. Reduce your expenses: Cut out unnecessary expenses and focus on needs over wants. You may need to make some lifestyle changes, but it will be worth it in the long run. 

4. Save money: Begin by setting aside a small amount of money each month, even if it is just $10. Once you have built up a savings, you can start investing it and watch it grow over time. 

5. Pay off your debt: High interest debt can be a huge burden. Try to pay off as much debt as possible, starting with the debt with the highest interest rate. 

6. Make a financial plan: Once you have a handle on your current financial situation, you can start planning for the future. Set goals and make a plan to achieve them. This will help you stay on track and motivated.
