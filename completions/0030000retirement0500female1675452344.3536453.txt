Assuming you would like tips for this person to improve their financial situation: 

Save: 
Start by building an emergency fund that can cover 3-6 months of living expenses. This will help you cover unexpected costs if you lose your job or have a major unexpected expense. Once you have this fund established, start saving for retirement. You can do this by contributing to a 401k or IRA.

Invest: 
Investing is a great way to grow your wealth over time. Consider investing in a mix of stocks, bonds, and mutual funds. Doing so will help you achieve your financial goals.

Live below your means: 
One of the best things you can do for your financial situation is to live below your means. This means spending less money than you make. Try to find ways to save money on your monthly expenses. For example, you can cook meals at home instead of eating out, purchase generic brands instead of name brands, and drive a less expensive car. 

Create a budget: 
Budgeting is crucial to managing your money. Track your income and expenses so you know where your money is going each month. This will help you make informed decisions about your spending and help you stick to your financial goals.
