First and foremost, it's important to get your debts under control. You should create a budget and make a plan to pay off your debts as quickly as possible. This may mean making some sacrifices in other areas of your life, but it will be worth it in the long run.

Once you have your debts under control, you should start to focus on saving money. Even if you can only save a little each month, it will add up over time. Try to put aside money each month to reach your financial goals.
