sex = female

returns from investment = 0
rent/month = 7000

give good comprehensive financial advice to such a person

==========

Assuming that this person would like to achieve her financial goal of starting a business: 

1. Invest in yourself: One of the best investments you can make is in yourself. Consider taking courses or attending workshops to help you develop the skills you need to start and grow your own business.

2. Save: Start by putting away as much money as you can each month into savings. Even if it’s just a small amount, it will add up over time and give you a nest egg to use for your business venture.

3. Create a budget: This will help you track your expenses and see where you can cut back in order to save more money. It will also give you a clear idea of how much money you will need to start your business.

4. Get organized: Make sure you have all the paperwork and documentation in order before you start reaching out to potential investors or taking out loans.

5. Research: Do your homework and research the industry you want to enter. Know your competition and what it will take to succeed.

6. Have a plan: Develop a business plan that includes your goals, strategies, and financial projections. This will help you make your case to potential investors and lenders.

7. Consider funding sources: In addition to savings, there are a number of ways to finance your business, such as loans, grants, and venture capital.Explore all your options and put together a plan for how you will raise the money you need.

8. Get started: Once you have a solid plan in place, it’s time to take the plunge and get started on your new business venture!
