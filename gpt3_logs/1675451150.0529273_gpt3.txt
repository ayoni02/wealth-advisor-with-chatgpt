Assuming this person wants comprehensive financial advice, here are a few key things to keep in mind:

1. Make sure to have an emergency fund saved up. This will help you cover unexpected costs in the event that something unexpected comes up.

2. Invest your money wisely. While there may not be a guarantee of returns, investing in things like stocks or mutual funds can give you the potential to earn more money down the line.

3. Try to keep your monthly expenses in check. It can be easy to let costs like eating out or buying new clothes eat into your budget, but try to limit these expenses as much as possible.

4. Make a plan for your financial goals. Whether you want to save up for a down payment on a house or retire early, Having a plan and setting aside money each month can help you achieve your goals.
