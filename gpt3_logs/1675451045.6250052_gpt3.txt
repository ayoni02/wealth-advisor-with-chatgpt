-creating a budget

The first step in getting your finances in order is to create a budget. Track your income and expenses for a month to get an idea of where your money is going. Be sure to include all fixed expenses, such as rent or mortgage payments, as well as variable expenses, such as food, transportation, and entertainment. Once you have a clear picture of your spending, you can begin to make changes to save money.

-Cutting costs

One of the easiest ways to save money is to cut costs. Evaluate your spending and see where you can cut back, even by a little bit. For example, if you spend $50 per week on lunch, try bringing your lunch from home a few days per week. This can save you hundreds of dollars per year. Similarly, if you have a gym membership that you never use, consider cancelling it. Every little bit helps when you are trying to save money.

-Saving money
