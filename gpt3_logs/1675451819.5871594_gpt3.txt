Assuming you would like general financial advice: 

1. It is always important to have some sort of savings, even if it is a small amount. Try to put away at least 10% of your salary into savings each month. This will help you in case of any unexpected expenses or financial goal. 

2. Try to invest your money in something that can give you a return. This can be done by research different options and comparing them. You may want to seek professional help to ensure you are making a wise decision. 

3. Make a budget and stick to it. This will help you track your monthly expenses and see where you may be able to cut back. Try to avoid any unnecessary expenses. 

4. Have a financial goal in mind. This could be saving for a car, a down payment on a house, or anything else you may want or need. Having a goal will help you stay motivated to save. 

5. Seek professional help if you feel you need it. There are many financial advisors and planners out there who can help you make wise decisions with your money.
