If you are a male with a salary of $10,000 and no returns from investments, your rent would be $5,000 per month and your monthly expenses would be $500. You would have $0 in savings and your financial goal would be to pay off debts.

Some tips to help you get started:

1. Review your expenses and see where you can cut back.

2. Make a budget and stick to it.

3. Invest in yourself by taking courses or learning about financial planning and investing.

4. Pay off your debts as soon as possible.

5. Save up an emergency fund to cover unexpected expenses.

6. Invest your money wisely to reach your financial goals.
