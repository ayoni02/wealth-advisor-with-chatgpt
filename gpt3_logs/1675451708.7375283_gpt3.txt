give good comprehensive financial advice to such a person

==========

Firstly, make sure that you have an emergency fund of at least 3-6 months of living expenses. This will help you cover unexpected costs in the event that you lose your job or face other unexpected financial challenges.

Next, work on building up your savings. You should aim to have at least 10% of your income saved each month. You can start by opening a high-yield savings account and automating your transfers.

Once you have a solid savings foundation, you can begin working towards your financial goals. In this case, you may want to consider taking out a home loan. Make sure to shop around for the best rate and remember to factor in all of the associated costs (e.g. insurance, repairs, etc.).

Finally, it is important to invest your money wisely. You can start by contributing to a 401(k) or IRA. If you have extra cash, you may want to consider investing in stocks, mutual funds, or real estate. However, it is important to do your research before investing any money.

In summary, it is important to have a strong financial foundation. This includes an emergency fund, savings, and a good investment strategy. Once you have this in place, you can begin working towards your financial goals.
