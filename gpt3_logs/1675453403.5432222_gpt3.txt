sex = male

returns from investment = 0
rent/month = 5000

give good comprehensive financial advice to such a person

==========

Assuming that this person is living paycheck to paycheck, the first thing I would advise them to do is to create a budget and stick to it. It is important to track where all of their money is going in order to make the necessary changes to save. For example, they may need to cut back on eating out or reduce their monthly expenses in order to have more money to put towards their savings.

I would also advise this person to start investing their money in order to reach their financial goals. For example, they could start saving for a down payment on a home by investing in a mutual fund or contributing to a 401k. By investing their money, they will be able to grow their savings at a faster rate than if they simply saved their money in a savings account.
