Assuming this person would like good comprehensive financial advice, some suggestions would be as follows:

1. Invest in a 401k or other retirement account:

Investing in a 401k or other retirement account is a good way to save for retirement and get tax benefits.

2. Invest in a 529 plan:

A 529 plan is a tax-advantaged savings plan designed to encourage savings for future education costs.

3. Save for emergencies:

It is important to have an emergency fund to cover unexpected costs in case of job loss, medical emergencies, or other unforeseen circumstances.

4. Make a budget:

Creating a budget can help you track your spending and figure out where you can save money.

5. Live below your means:

Living below your means means spending less than you earn and saving the rest. This can help you build up your savings and reach your financial goals.
