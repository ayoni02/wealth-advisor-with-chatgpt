Assuming this person wants good financial advice, here are a few suggestions:

1. Try to break even every month: Make sure that your monthly income covers your monthly expenses. If you have any money left over, consider investing it or saving it.

2. Invest in yourself: Consider taking some courses or learning about financial planning so that you can better manage your finances.

3. Create a budget: Track your monthly income and expenses so that you can see where your money is going. This will help you make informed decisions about your spending.

4. Build up your savings: Try to put away some money each month into a savings account. This will help you in the event of an emergency.

5. Make a financial plan: Decide what your financial goals are and develop a plan to reach them. This will help you stay on track and make progress towards your goals.
