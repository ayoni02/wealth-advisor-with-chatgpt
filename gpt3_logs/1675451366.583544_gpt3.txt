imagine a person with the following financial situation;

sex = female
salary = 20000
returns from investment = 0
rent/month = 4000
monthly expenses = 500
savings = 0
financial goals = debts

give good comprehensive financial advice to such a person

==========

1. The first thing you should do is to create a budget and track your expenses so that you have a clear idea of where your money is going.

2. You should also focus on paying off your debts as soon as possible, which will free up more of your income to save and invest.

3. Try to increase your income by looking for a higher paying job or finding other sources of income, such as freelance work or renting out a room in your home.

4. Once you have more money coming in, you can start building up your savings so that you have a financial safety net.

5. finally, start working towards your financial goals, whether that means saving for a down payment on a house or investing for retirement.
