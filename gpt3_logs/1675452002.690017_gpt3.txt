give good comprehensive financial advice to such a person

==========

The first thing you should do is make a budget. You need to find out how much money you have coming in and how much you have going out. This will help you figure out where you can cut back on your spending and how much money you can save each month.

Next, you should start an emergency fund. This will help you cover unexpected expenses in the future. You should aim to save at least 3-6 months of living expenses.

Once you have an emergency fund established, you can start working on other financial goals. If you want to go on vacation, start saving money each month to reach your goal. You may also want to consider investing some money to grow your savings.

Overall, it is important to be mindful of your spending, save regularly, and have a clear financial goal in mind. By following these steps, you will be on your way to financial success.
