Assuming this person wants good financial advice, here are a few things they could do:

- Save: Try to put away at least 10% of your salary into savings. This will help you build up a cushion in case of unforeseen expenses or financial setbacks.

- Invest: Consider investing some of your savings in stocks, mutual funds, or other vehicles that have the potential to generate returns. This can help you grow your wealth over time.

- Budget: Make sure to track your monthly income and expenses so that you can stay mindful of where your money is going. This can help you curb unnecessary spending and make sure you are on track to reach your financial goals.

- Education: If one of your financial goals is to save for education, there are a few things you can do to make that more affordable. Consider taking classes online or at a community college to save on tuition costs. You may also be able to get scholarships or financial aid to help with expenses.
