Assuming this person is looking for general financial advice, here are a few tips:

1. Invest in yourself - one of the best investments you can make is in your own education and career. Invest in yourself by taking courses, attending conferences, and reading books and articles to stay up-to-date in your field.

2. Make a budget and stick to it - knowing exactly where your money is going each month is key to financial success. Make a budget and track your spending so that you can make adjustments as needed. Try using a budgeting app or tool to help you stay on track.

3. Save, save, save - it’s never too early to start saving for your future. Begin by setting aside money each month into a savings account. Automating your savings can help make it easier to reach your financial goals.

4. Invest for the long term - in general, the stock market has been shown to provide good long-term growth. If you’re comfortable taking on some risk, consider investing a portion of your savings into stocks or mutual funds.

5. Live below your means - one of the best ways to stay financially afloat is to make sure your spending doesn’t exceed your income. Consider ways to cut costs in your budget so that you can save more and avoid debt.

By following these tips, you can begin to build a solid financial foundation that will help you reach your financial goals.
