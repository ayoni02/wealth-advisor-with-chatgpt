imagine a person with the following financial situation;

sex = male
salary = 30000
returns from investment = 0
rent/month = 9000
monthly expenses = 500
savings = 0
financial goals = debts

give good comprehensive financial advice to such a person

==========

The first thing you need to do is create a budget. Track your income and expenses for one month to get an idea of where your money is going. Once you have a budget, you can start working on reducing your expenses. Try to find ways to reduce your monthly expenses by cutting back on non-essential items.

Next, you need to focus on paying off your debts. Make a list of all your debts, starting with the highest interest rate first. Create a budget that includes debt payments and stick to it. You may need to make some sacrifices in order to reach your financial goals, but it will be worth it in the end.

Finally, you should start saving for your future. Even if you can only save a small amount each month, it will add up over time. Begin by setting aside money for emergencies, and then start working towards longer-term goals like retirement.

If you follow these steps, you will be on your way to financial success.
