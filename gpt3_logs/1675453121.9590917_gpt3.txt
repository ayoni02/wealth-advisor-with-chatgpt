==========

Good comprehensive financial advice for such a person would include the following: 

1. Review your expenses and make changes where necessary. Try to cut down on unnecessary expenses and redirect that money into savings.

2. Try to invest your money into something that will give you a return. Look into different options and speak with a financial advisor to see what would be the best fit for you.

3. Make sure to have a solid emergency fund saved up. This will help you cover unexpected costs in the event that something unexpected comes up.

4. Stay disciplined with your spending and savings. Try to put away as much money as possible each month so that you can reach your financial goals.
