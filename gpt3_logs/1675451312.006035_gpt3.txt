give good comprehensive financial advice to such a person

==========

Make a budget: 

The first step is to make a budget. Determine how much money you bring in each month and what your regular expenses are. This will help you figure out how much money you have available to save each month.

Save: 

Save as much money as you can each month. Even if it’s just a little bit, it will add up over time. You can open a savings account and have a portion of your paycheck deposited into it automatically.

Invest: 

Investing is a great way to grow your money. Consider opening a brokerage account and investing in a mix of stocks, bonds, and other investments.

Cut expenses: 

Take a close look at your expenses and see if there are any areas where you can cut back. For example, you may be able to save money on your monthly car payment by downsizing to a less expensive vehicle.

Build up your emergency fund: 

An emergency fund is a crucial part of your financial plan. It will help you cover unexpected expenses, like a car repair or a medical bill. Aim to save enough money to cover three to six months of living expenses.

Pay off debt: 

If you have high-interest debt, like credit card debt, it’s important to focus on paying it off as quickly as possible. Try to make more than the minimum payment each month and consider transferring your balance to a lower-interest credit card.

Create a financial plan: 

A financial plan will help you meet your long-term financial goals. It will give you a roadmap to follow and can help keep you on track when things get tough.
