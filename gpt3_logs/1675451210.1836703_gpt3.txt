Assuming this person is looking for general financial advice, here are a few key pieces of advice that could help them get on track:

1. Make a budget: One of the most important things you can do when it comes to your finances is to create and stick to a budget. This will help you keep track of your income and expenses so that you can make informed decisions about how to best use your money.

2. Invest in yourself: One of the best investments you can make is in yourself. This can include things like furthering your education or taking courses to improve your job prospects. Investing in yourself will help you earn more money over the long term, which can then be used to improve your financial situation.

3. Save money: Another key piece of financial advice is to make sure you are saving money each month. Even if it is just a small amount, putting money into savings can help you build up a cushion that can be used in case of emergencies or unexpected expenses.

4. Make smart choices with your money: When it comes to your finances, it is important to make smart choices with your money. This can include things like investing in quality products that will last long-term, or avoiding unnecessary purchases that can add up over time.

5. Seek professional help: If you are feeling overwhelmed by your finances, it may be helpful to seek professional help. A financial advisor can offer guidance and advice on how to best manage your money, and can help you develop a plan to achieve your financial goals.
