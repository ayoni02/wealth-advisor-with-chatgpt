imagine a person with the following financial situation;

sex = male
salary = 20000
returns from investment = 0
rent/month = 4000
monthly expenses = 500
savings = 0
financial goals = education

give good comprehensive financial advice to such a person

==========

.

The first step is to save money. Try to put away at least 10% of your salary into savings each month. If you can do more, great. If you have any returns from investments, put that into savings as well. The goal is to have at least 3-6 months of living expenses saved so that you have a cushion in case of job loss or other unexpected expenses.

Next, focus on your financial goals. If you want to save for education, start researching ways to do that. Look into scholarships, loans, and other ways to finance your education. Create a budget and make sure that you are putting away enough each month to reach your goal.

Finally, make sure that you are living within your means. That means not spending more money than you make and not racking up credit card debt. If you can stick to a budget and make smart financial choices, you will be on your way to a bright financial future.
