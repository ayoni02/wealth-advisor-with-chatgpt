Assuming this person wants good comprehensive financial advice, here are a few suggestions:

1. Save: The first step to good financial planning is saving your money. Try to put away at least 10-15% of your income into savings. This will help you in the long run when it comes to saving for retirement or other financial goals.

2. Invest: Another important step is to invest your money. This can be done through a variety of methods, such as stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and real estate. By investing, you can grow your wealth over time and reach your financial goals sooner.

3. Budget: It is also important to create a budget and stick to it. This will help you keep track of your spending and make sure you are not overspending. A budget will also help you save money each month.

4. Reduce Debt: Another good piece of advice is to reduce your debt. This can be done by paying off your credit cards, car loans, and any other debts you may have. By reducing your debt, you will have more money each month to save and invest.

5. Financial Goals: Lastly, it is important to set financial goals. By setting goals, you will have something to work towards. Make sure your goals are realistic and achievable. Once you reach your goals, you can then set new ones.
